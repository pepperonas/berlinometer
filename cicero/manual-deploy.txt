Manual Deployment Steps for Cicero:

1. SSH into your VPS:
   ssh root@mrx3k1.de

2. Create the directory if it doesn't exist:
   mkdir -p /var/www/html/cicero/frontend

3. On your local machine, from the cicero/frontend directory:
   rsync -avz --delete build/ root@mrx3k1.de:/var/www/html/cicero/frontend/

4. Or use scp:
   scp -r build/* root@mrx3k1.de:/var/www/html/cicero/frontend/

5. Set correct permissions on the server:
   chown -R www-data:www-data /var/www/html/cicero/
   chmod -R 755 /var/www/html/cicero/

The frontend files should now be deployed and accessible at https://mrx3k1.de/cicero/
